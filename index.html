About

Sam Padrul is a Chicago-based musician with fifteen years of crafting music that moves. Drawing from the golden grooves of disco, the sleek textures of French house, and the sun-soaked ease of yacht rock, his sound sits at the intersection of danceable and timeless — music that feels both familiar and fresh. Whether behind the boards or performing live, Sam brings a deep love for rhythm, melody, and the moments that only music can create.
